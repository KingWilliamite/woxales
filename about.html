My Thoughts on Life

On Building Brands

Building a brand is not just about creating a logo and a website. Nor is it about the financial opportunities it brings. Your goal as a founder is to focus on a vision mission so grandiose it outlasts your lifetime. You want to build a brand that is not just a company, but a movement. A movement that is so powerful it transcends generations. A movement that is so powerful it changes the world forever.

On Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are not just animals. They are a symbol of the past, the present, and the future. They are a reminder that we are not alone in this universe. They are a reminder that we are all part of the same journey. They are a reminder that we are all part of the same story.
